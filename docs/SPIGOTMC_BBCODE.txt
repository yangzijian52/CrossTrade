[B]Overview[/B]

CrossTrade provides secure player-to-player trading and a persistent player marketplace for Paper 26.2 servers. It keeps direct trading available while adding a market plaza, player shops, listings, purchase confirmation, expiration handling, safe item returns, seller payouts, mailbox recovery and transaction history.

The plugin is designed around safety. It validates money and inventory state before finalizing trades, keeps market inventory in SQLite, avoids dropping recovery items on the ground and records uncertain payout/refund states for administrator review.

[B]Main Features[/B]

[LIST]
[*]Direct player trading with request cooldowns, expiry handling and secure confirmation.
[*]Remote online-player trade entry from the market center.
[*]54-slot direct trade GUI with symmetric item areas and configurable amount buttons.
[*]Amount controls include +1, +10, +100, +1000 and -1, -10, -100, -1000.
[*]Player market plaza grouped by seller.
[*]Player shop pages with real ItemStack icons, pagination and purchase confirmation.
[*]Listing flow with item selection, market name, quantity, unit price and 1-10 day duration.
[*]Partial purchases are supported.
[*]Expired, cancelled and removed listings return remaining items safely through the mailbox.
[*]Mailbox system for safe item recovery when inventories are full or players are offline.
[*]Seller payout ledger with pending, paying, paid and review states.
[*]Transaction history with player-side clear display support.
[*]Restock and relist flows for inactive listings.
[*]SQLite storage with schema migration, WAL and consistent backup command.
[*]Configurable GUI pages through gui.yml.
[*]Vault and Floodgate are soft dependencies.
[/LIST]

[B]Installation[/B]

[LIST=1]
[*]Install Java 25 and Paper 26.2.
[*]Install Vault and a compatible economy plugin if you want money transactions.
[*]Install Floodgate only if you need Bedrock form support.
[*]Place CrossTrade-2.2.1.jar in the server plugins folder.
[*]Start the server once to generate config.yml, gui.yml and data.db.
[*]Review plugins/CrossTrade/config.yml and plugins/CrossTrade/gui.yml.
[*]Back up plugins/CrossTrade before upgrading from older versions.
[/LIST]

[B]Data and Safety[/B]

[LIST]
[*]Database: plugins/CrossTrade/data.db
[*]Backups: plugins/CrossTrade/backups/
[*]Audit log: plugins/CrossTrade/logs/transactions.log
[*]Config: plugins/CrossTrade/config.yml
[*]GUI config: plugins/CrossTrade/gui.yml
[/LIST]

Items are stored using Paper ItemStack byte serialization and SHA-256 fingerprints. Shulker boxes and other container-like items keep their contents as part of the item data. Items that cannot be returned to a player's inventory are stored in the mailbox instead of being dropped into the world.

[B]Dependencies[/B]

Vault is a soft dependency so the plugin can load without it, but purchases, payouts and money-based direct trades require Vault and a working economy provider. Floodgate is optional and only used for Bedrock forms. Java-player functionality remains available without Floodgate.

[B]Known Limits[/B]

[LIST]
[*]Built and tested for Paper 26.2. Other server implementations are not claimed as supported.
[*]Do not use /reload or third-party hot unload tools for this plugin.
[*]Vault economy plugins differ in offline-player payout behavior; CrossTrade keeps failed payouts in a safe ledger for later handling.
[*]The SpigotMC support channel is English-only. Chinese-language support is not provided on SpigotMC.
[/LIST]
